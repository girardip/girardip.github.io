Profitability per product line estimation

  • Where: Freelancing, American Italian tourism company
  • Problem: client did not know the labor costs of each product, limiting strategical decision-making
  • Solution: I estimated labor costs across product lines. Moreover, I identified a gap in the knowledge of physical asset costs (e.g., vans and bikes). Once I calculated the costs and profitability per product line, I showed some of the growth bets of the company and its largest B2B client were actually not profitable.
  • Skills: Excel modeling, interviews with 15+ employees

Implementation of AdTech Product

  • Where: Delivery Hero (Germany), working directly with LATAM, APAC, and MENA regional teams
  • Problem: A new AdTech product was the biggest bet of the year, but did not generate any growth.
  • Solution: I project managed an investigation led by the VP of Global Sales and Revenue. I partnered with local analytics teams to test our product and identify technical issues and sales gaps. After critical technical changes and improved sales guidance, the product grew from €3MM to €10MM within 12 months.

Go-to-market strategy

  • Where: BCG US, Fortune 500 client
  • Problem: Our client, a major distributor, wanted advice on the negotiation of a new contract with a business partner (group purchasing organization)
  • Solution: I analyzed transaction data to run pre-post model on the impact of partnership on sales and profitability. The analyses showed that the partnership had significantly hurt our client’s bottom line. I designed the quantitative part of our presentation for CEO, who chose to follow our recommendations. We estimated savings at $118MM over the duration of the contract.
  • Skills: Excel modeling, data manipulation, stakeholder interviews
